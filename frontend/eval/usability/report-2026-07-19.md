### Harness / evaluator observations (not product defects)

- **Two misreads per task is the noise floor, not signal.** `llama3.1:8b` produced 16 "misreads" for 8 tasks — a near-constant two each — including on the two tasks it fully understood. This is precisely why the harness is a report, not a CI gate, and why every row is triaged by hand. With Claude as the evaluator (set `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`) the signal-to-noise would be far higher; re-running there is the obvious next improvement.
- **Section captures include the page header stats.** `cancerSection()` concatenates `header` + the stat `dl` + the section body, so the associated-targets subline (`score ≥ 0.5 · 12,475 …`) appears in the text for *every* cancer-section task and gets re-flagged on unrelated ones (survival). Consider dropping the header from section captures so each task's text is scoped to its block — otherwise the same stat keeps re-entering triage.
- **`completed` is unreliable on this model.** Two tasks the reader actually understood (source-failed, arguably osimertinib-potency) were scored `no`/`partially`. Trust the `answer` and `termsMisread` fields over the `completed` flag when triaging a small model.
